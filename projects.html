Where R U?

Where R U is an application meant to make the creation and management of carpools easier. Everyone has an experience where a bunch of people are all trying to get to one place, and they want to carpool there to save gas, but for whatever reason everything just seems to fall apart. We hope to help solve this issue.

Essentially, what we want to do is make it so that you can make carpool groups, which will either be one time or recurring, and then allow the driver to notify the passengers when he/she is leaving. The passengers will then let the driver know that they are ready to be picked up, and our app will get their locations and plot a route through Google Maps (or Apple Maps on iOS) to get to each passenger and then the destination.

This app is an RCOS project, and is still in heavy development.

RRS Payload

As a member of the Rensselaer Rocket Society, I have decided to work on our scientific payload. This device will collect data as our rocket flies, and will transmit this data down to us in real time. We will use the data to create an accurate model of the rocket in flight, and anything else we think of doing before the flight.
